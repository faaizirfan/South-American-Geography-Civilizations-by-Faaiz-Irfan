🧑‍🤠 2. The First Peoples of South America

The first people in South America were really smart! They built cool cities, farmed food, and made awesome art. They lived in the mountains, forests, and deserts.

🏛️ 3. Ancient Civilizations

🌞 The Mayans

The Mayans made huge pyramids, created a super-smart calendar, and were great at math!

🦙 The Inca Civilization

The Incas built their homes in the mountains, made cool stone roads, and created the amazing Machu Picchu!

🌍 4. Impact on Modern Culture

Even today, we still use ideas from these ancient people! Their farming, art, and buildings inspire us.

🏆 5. Contributions That Impressed Me

  • 📅 Mayan Calendar: They figured out time better than anyone back then!
  • 🌾 Inca Terraced Farming: They farmed on mountains in a super smart way!
  • 🎨 Aztec Art: Their statues and carvings tell awesome stories!

Did you know? The Atacama Desert in South America is one of the driest places on Earth!
